# Airport Authority

A lens, not a reviewer. Captures the entity that owns and develops the airport.

## Primary concerns

- Capacity and capital funding (AIP/PFC) for expansion (DIM-02/12).
- Community relations on noise and land use (DIM-10).
- Carrier diversity and non-aeronautical revenue (DIM-07).

## Used during

- Capacity, benefit-cost, and environmental scoring; tier/SLA sponsorship review.
