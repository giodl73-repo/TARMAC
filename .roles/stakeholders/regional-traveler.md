# Regional Traveler

A lens, not a reviewer. Captures travelers from small and mid-size markets dependent on
connecting service.

## Primary concerns

- Nonstop or one-stop access to where they need to go (DIM-04/06).
- Fare levels under hub competition (DIM-07).
- Reliable connections that do not strand them (DIM-03).

## Used during

- Connectivity, access, and delay scoring; tier/SLA for regional service (T3).
