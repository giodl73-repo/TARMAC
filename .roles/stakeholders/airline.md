# Airline

A lens, not a reviewer. Captures the carrier deciding what to fly and where to hub.

- Slot and gate access at constrained airports (DIM-09).
- Route profitability, demand, and load factor (DIM-01/12).
- Operational reliability and recovery (DIM-03/05).

## Used during

- Slot, competition, and delay scoring; tier/SLA feasibility with the slot-and-hub realist.
