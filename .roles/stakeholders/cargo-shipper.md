# Air-Cargo / Logistics Shipper

A lens, not a reviewer. Captures cargo and express-logistics users dependent on air freight
hubs and reliable lift.

## Primary concerns

- Freighter slot/ramp access and off-peak capacity (DIM-02/09).
- Reliability and recovery for time-definite freight (DIM-03/05).
- Intermodal/ground connection to distribution (DIM-08).

## Used during

- Capacity, reliability, and intermodal scoring; tier/SLA for cargo at hubs (T1/T2).
