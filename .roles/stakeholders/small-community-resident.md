# Small-Community Resident

A lens, not a reviewer. Captures residents of remote/small communities for whom air
service is a lifeline, not a convenience.

## Primary concerns

- Any maintained scheduled service / Essential Air Service (DIM-06).
- Drive distance to the nearest real airport (DIM-04/11).
- Medical, emergency, and business access (DIM-06).

## Used during

- Access and equity scoring; tier/SLA for local/GA and essential service (T4).
